Account & Data Deletion

Google Play requires that apps offering user accounts provide a clear mechanism for users to request deletion of their account and all associated personal data.

  • By Email: Send a deletion request to leeward192@gmail.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request"

We will process deletion requests within 30 days. Some data may be retained for a limited period if required by law (e.g. financial transaction records for tax or legal compliance), after which it will be permanently deleted or anonymized.
